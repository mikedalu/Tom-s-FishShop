Our History

For many years, Tom has been making the most delicious fish dishes in Jos. In 2011, we opened our first joint in Kano (Nigeria) close to one of the most desired hotel in Kano. It was a huge success within just three months of operation and people from all over the country fell in love with the different varieties of dishes made.
In 2018, no less than 100 plates of fish were sold every day and Tom's joint decided it was time to take things to the next level - hence the creation of this website. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the country. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order Tom's delicious fishes anytime and anywhere!

Tom is famous for using only the best ingredients found in Jos. This way, we can make sure that our fishes are absolutely the best you can find anywhere.
